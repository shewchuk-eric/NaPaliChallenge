The Course

The 37 mile course starts on the beach at Hanalei Bay on Kauai's north shore. The mens team will start the race and paddle for the first 30 minutes before the womens crew takes over. During the first round of paddling, most teams will make it out of the bay and travel as far as five miles to the west.

For the next few hours, the teams will alternate between resting and paddling in 30 minute increments. Top teams typically maintain a pace of about 7 miles per hour when paddling a Traditional class canoe. Speeds are closer to 9 miles per hour for top teams paddling Unlimited class canoes. This means that most paddling teams will cross the finish line in 4-6 hours after the start.
